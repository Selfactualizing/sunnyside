I'm Sarah!

Sarah J. Boulton, MA, CCC-SLP is an innovative and creative Speech-Language Pathologist with over 20 years of experience in the fields of Music Therapy and Speech-Language Pathology.

She’s from the Midwest where she received her Bachelor’s in Music Therapy in 1993 and a Master’s in Speech Language Pathology in 2000 from the University of Kansas. She has worked in a variety of settings with special needs children and adults to provide individualized and group Speech and Music Therapy.

Although Speech and Language Communication is the primary focus, a multimodality approach is implemented to maximize the full capacity of each child. Her services provide a holistic and natural setting to help a child feel at ease to increase learning potential. A whole child, mind-body-brain approach is implemented where movement, music, arts, and emotions are integrated in age appropriate play interventions. Parent involvement and training are emphasized to provide a comprehensive creative programming that promotes generalization through all environments.

 Please call or email Sarah to discuss your individual child’s needs.

SunnySide Speech Therapy, LLC is a pediatric private practice located in Tucker, GA (ITP) NE greater Atlanta area.

Our mission is to provide an innovative, multi-modality approach to learning through child centered play that includes, music, movement, sensory integration techniques and creative arts. Each child will be provided with a comprehensive evaluation, individualized therapy and appropriate resources to aid in achieving speech, language and feeding goals in a caring and supportive environment.  


Treatment Services include:
• Oral-Motor
• Feeding and Swallowing safety
• Articulation/phonological
• Receptive/Expressive Language
• Childhood Apraxia of Speech
• Neurological and Medical delays
• Developmental Delays
• Late Talker
• Preemie Infant global delays
• Stuttering/fluency
• Reading Delays
• Voice disorders
